Boba

Boba is prepared from cassava root and has a chewy, gummy texture after cooking. We utilize "Grade A" boba imported straight from Taiwan. To retain the freshness and texture of our boba, it is cooked fresh throughout the day. Our boba is prepared in our kitchen and has a 4-hour shelf life to ensure it has a chewy consistency with a sweet honey flavor. It may be mixed into any beverage to take your drinking experience to the next level. Once you drink itâ€¦.it can be hard to stop.

Premium Teas

Our teas are freshly brewed loose leaves throughout the day, and we brew them for a particular period before mixing them into drinks to achieve the most refreshing flavor. We wanted to provide our tea enthusiasts the choice to try traditional teas other than green or black tea.

Mission Statement


Boba Tea N' Me's team enjoys serving consumers and giving back to the community via our excellent services. We wish to provide our customers a place where they can spend quality time with their families, friends, and colleagues. Every day, we strive to provide our customers with more premium products and healthier alternatives. It is our duty to service our community by supporting them achieving their own mission.

Boba

Boba is prepared from cassava root and has a chewy, gummy texture after cooking. We utilize "Grade A" boba imported straight from Taiwan. To retain the freshness and texture of our boba, it is cooked fresh throughout the day. Our boba is prepared in our kitchen and has a 4-hour shelf life to ensure it has a chewy consistency with a sweet honey flavor. Premium Teas

Our teas are freshly brewed loose leaves throughout the day, and we brew them for a particular period before mixing them into drinks to achieve the most refreshing flavor. We wanted to provide our tea enthusiasts the choice to try traditional teas other than green or black tea.
